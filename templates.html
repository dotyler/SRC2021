About SRC

The South Regional Conference (SRC) 2021 is a collaboration of the South Central Regional Conference (SCRC) and Southeast Regional Conference (SERC) with 10+ years of regional conference planning experience.

Over 400 students and professionals from throughout the South Region had registered for SERC 2020 and SCRC 2020 to network and participate in our workshops. We aim to foster professional development, diversity, and service to all our participants.

Registration for SRC 2020!

For 2021, we will be combining the regional conferences of South Central and South East regions of SASE to host SASE South Regional Conference! Fill out our Google Form and submit your registration fees.
