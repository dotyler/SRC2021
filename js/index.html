Frequently Asked Questions

Here is a list of question that you may ask about the SASE South Regional Conference.

  • When is South Regional Conference?

    South Regional Conference will hosted Virtually by UF on March 19-20th 2021. We will be using Zoom and Brazen as our platforms for this conference.

  • What can I expect from the conference?

    By attending the conference, you will be able to improve your soft and hard skills and build new, meaningful relationships with both professions and fellow students. The conference is a platform for you to showcase your skills and to advance your career.

  • What should I bring to the conference?

    To attend the conference, a computer with wifi abilities is required as the conference this year is entirely online. It is advised that you have a notepad and your resume handy as you interact with recruiters and fellow students. In addition, business casual attire is recommended to maintain a professionalism at the conference.

  • This is my first virtual conference. Is there anything I should note?

    As the conference is held virtually this year, it will be different from physical conferences held in previous years. Please make sure to check the schedule regularly as to not miss any important events. In addition, feel free to join the Discord for updates during the conference. In addition, there are resources made available that will aid you during the conference.
